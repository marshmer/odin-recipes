Pizzaghetti

Pizza and pasta combined to make pizzaghetti!

This dish combines the savory pepperonis from a pizza and the buttery taste of angel hair pasta into an easy to bake dish the whole family will love.

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Look spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite. Drain and transfer to a 7x11 inch baking dish.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  3. Stir spaghetti sauce into pasta and add 2 layers of pepperoni, olives, and mushrooms on top. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil.
  4. Bake about 20 mins, uncover and sprinkle with mozzarella cheese, and return to the oven to bake for another 10 minutes.
