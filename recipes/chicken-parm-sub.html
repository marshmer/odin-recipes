Chicken Parm Sub

An Italian Classic

Chicken combined with bread, hearty pasta sauce, and gooey provolone and parmesan cheeses to make an Italian staple on the go!

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F, and spray baking pan with vegetable oil spray.
  2. Place frozen chicken fillets on pan and bake for 10-12 minutes. Turn chicken, sprinkle 2 tablespoons of parmesan cheese on each fillet, and bake until crispy (10-12 mins).
  3. Slice garlic bread into 4 equal portions. Open each portion and place garlic side up on a baking sheet. Warm garlic bread in the oven about 5 mins.
  4. To assemble, place a chicken fillet on a piece of garlic bread, spoon 1/4 cup tomato sauce or marinara sauce on top of the chicken, and lay a slice of provolone on top of the sauce. Place 3 basil leaves, or more to taste, on each sandwich, and top with the other half.
  5. Wrap each sandwich in foil and return to the oven until the cheese is melted, 5-8 mins. Unwrap and serve warm.
